**Format convention:**

Entries should be short, specific, and self-contained. Bad:

> "Short-form video is important for growth."

Good:

> "YouTube Shorts drive 93% of channel traffic; long-form under 100 views
> since 2026-03. Focus new uploads on Shorts until Subs > 1000."

Each entry should encode a fact, a number, and (ideally) an actionable
implication. Vague generalities are noise; specific patterns are signal.
